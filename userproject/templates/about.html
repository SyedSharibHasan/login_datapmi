About Us

DataPMI is a leading Data Analytics and Technology solutions company with a strong presence in India, including offices in Bangalore and Kochi, and its Corporate Headquarters located in Texas, USA.

Our mission at DataPMI is to generate business value by identifying opportunities where cutting-edge technologies such as Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Advanced Algorithms (AA) can be seamlessly integrated with other Data Science capabilities. We focus on optimizing business processes through data-driven, analytics-driven decision-making approaches. Our team designs supervised machine learning models that learn from historical data, enabling accurate predictions on new, unseen data and facilitating scenario analysis for future projections. Additionally, we develop innovative unsupervised machine learning algorithms for scenarios where verifiable historical data is unavailable, offering valuable insights autonomously. Embracing Deep Learning methodologies, we incorporate "human-like" learning concepts, advancing our capabilities in Artificial Intelligence. Our expertise in Natural Language Processing allows us to analyze unstructured data, including clinical trial descriptions, patent claims, and social media content, enhancing our portfolio and providing a competitive advantage to our clients.

At DataPMI, we specialize in software development and offer comprehensive information technology solutions to a diverse range of companies. Our expertise spans specialized areas such as project management implementation, eCommerce, Enterprise Performance Management (EPM), Corporate Performance Management (CPM), database administration, and internet/intranet web applications. Moreover, we excel in Analytics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and application engineering. We collaborate with renowned technology partners such as Databricks, Anaplan, Board, Tableau, PowerBI, and Automation Anywhere to deliver cutting-edge solutions to our clients.

Expertise and Specializations: DataPMI excels in various domains, including:

• Advanced Data Analytics : Leveraging advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques, DataPMI provides in-depth data analysis, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions with confidence.

• Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence : DataPMI pioneers in the field of ML and AI, crafting sophisticated algorithms that learn and adapt, delivering predictive analytics and valuable insights.

• Natural Language Processing (NLP) : The company's expertise in NLP allows for the analysis of unstructured data, extracting meaningful information from sources like clinical trial descriptions and social media, providing valuable business intelligence.

• Information Technology Solutions : From database administration to internet/intranet web applications, DataPMI provides comprehensive IT solutions, ensuring smooth operations and robust cybersecurity measures.

• Collaboration with Technology Partners : DataPMI collaborates with renowned technology partners like Databricks, Anaplan, Board, Tableau, PowerBI, and Automation Anywhere. These partnerships enhance their capabilities and allow them to deliver cutting-edge solutions.
